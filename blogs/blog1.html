How to win Friend and influence people

How to win Friend and influence people is a book of social dynamics. Dale Carnegie has compiled his best advices from his work and study of people about how to make people like you and be unforgettable.

Here our top 5 Lessons from the books.

Lesson 1 Don't complain, everyone is right in their minds:-
Nobody thinks they are stupid. Everyone is right in their own mind so by complaining and making them feel that they are wrong you will get what you want. Instead make the complain sound like an advice or suggestion instead being direct on the face.

Lesson 2 People want to feel important anyway.
Compliments always work. Everyone wants to feel good about themselves. All of us are insecure about something and lil bit of appreciation goes a long way, making the other person feel they are important.

Lesson 3 Everyone is interested in themselves so for them to like you, you should be interested in them.
Most of us like to talk about ourselves when given the right and comfortable environment. Be that person who is a good listener or atleast be interested to know about the other person. It will significantly increase you likability for the other person.

Lesson 4 Smile, yes it is obvious but we forget.
You can never ignore a nice smile. It is in human to mirror others emotions. So Yes SMILE more!
